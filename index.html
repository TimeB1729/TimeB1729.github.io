About

I'm Shyam Banerjee, a B.Stat undergraduate at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. My research interests sit where rigorous statistics meets the natural world — building probabilistic models for astrophysical data, understanding the structure of financial volatility, and exploring what Bayesian inference has to say about deeply uncertain systems.

In summer 2025 I worked with the AstroStatistics group at Penn State on GP-based imputation for Kepler mission data, leading to my first authored paper. I write essays on statistical intuition at Mindspace and keep a technical notebook at Codespace.
