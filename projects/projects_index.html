Astrostatistics Manuscript

GP-based Imputation in Kepler Stellar Light Curves

A Gaussian Process framework for imputing missing photometric observations in Kepler stellar light curves. The study evaluates a quiet star and an active rotating star, demonstrating the GP approach outperforms classical deterministic interpolation. Conducted as independent research following the AstroStatistics summer programme at Penn State.
